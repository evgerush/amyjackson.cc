In reaction to the previous three paintings, which were giving Jackson
heavy flashbacks from what she saw of the Viet-Nam War on TV from 1964-1975
as a child, she created three Scream paintings. This is a screaming flower.
It is sadly, bruised, brutalized and shocked, crying out in pain in the midst
of the war and the jungle surrounding it. Jackson describes this scream as
something she can hear but not utter; only paint.

Viet-Nam War IV
Acrylic on canvas
22 x 28 inches
