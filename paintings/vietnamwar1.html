This is an overall scene of war in Viet-Nam in all its chaos and unpredictability.
This painting also sets the key code for the palette for the series. Black = Death.
Red = Blood shed by anyone in the conflict. Greens = Jungle foliage and/or combat
camouflage. Sky Blue =  Peace or attempts at peace, infinity or sky.
Gray = Officers, Institutions, smoke or clouds. Yellow = Energy or Fire.
