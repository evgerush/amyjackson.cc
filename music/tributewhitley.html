Amy Jackson
___ Silhouette: A Tribute to Chris Whitley ___

http://www.chriswhitley.com/

Lush and drippy, Amy's voice wraps around the chords and the beats with a slow...easy, hot and sweaty sensuality. This woozy bluesy songstress is also an incredibly talented visual artist. Check out her music and the art on her website or be a fool...your choice. -- Robert Tucker, www.indizoo.com
