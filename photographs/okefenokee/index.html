We were in Folkston, Georgia for three days, in order to take these photographs in the Okefenokee National Wildlife Refuge.

who took us on a five-hour tour, and thank you to Joy for making all of our arrangements.

We stayed at the Inn at Folkston,
and had a delightful experience feeling at home there for a while.
