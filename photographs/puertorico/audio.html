Coquis

Amy Jackson © 2007

The frog that you hear is the Coqui, much loved
by the Puerto Rican people (and now by us),
in part because they help eat mosquitos
and other biting insects. The multitude of lizards
also help with this, and the mongooses
eat the snakes. All in all, a great ecosystem.
