Blinds

We just happened to see these because we had stopped to photograph an old rusty unusual machine on the opposite block. I think they're dreamy.
They belong to an old office building there, just window blinds, but ancient and retro and like turquoise waters ... I imagine some office workers drifting off
in the afternoons watching them weave in out and out of the light, shadow and circulating air and heat ... Nashville, TN.
